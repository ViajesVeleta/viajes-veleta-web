**Getaria** is one of the most charming villages on the Cantabrian coast. The birthplace of great historical figures, it saw the birth of Juan Sebastián Elcano —who led the first circumnavigation of the globe— and the legendary designer **Cristóbal Balenciaga**. His high-fashion museum is now a must-stop for art and fashion lovers.

But Getaria offers much more: its medieval layout, its fishing port, and a vibrant winemaking tradition. It is here where **txakoli** with a designation of origin is produced, a young and fruity white wine that pairs perfectly with the sea breeze. A Basque gem that we will discover on our next group trip in June.
