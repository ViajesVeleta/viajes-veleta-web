Crowned by its Alcazaba and overlooking the Mediterranean, **Malaga** has established itself as a benchmark for modern tourism. Recognized as the European Capital of Smart Tourism, the city stands out for its commitment to accessibility, sustainability, and digitalization, without losing a bit of its historical essence.

Cultural spaces like the Picasso Museum coexist with an iconic gastronomy marked by the famous *espeto* (sardine skewer). One of the best views of this urban evolution can be seen from the Gibralfaro Castle, overlooking the vibrant **Muelle Uno** (Pier One) of the harbor, a symbol of a Malaga that looks to the future with creativity and respect for its heritage.
