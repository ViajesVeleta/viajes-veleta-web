**New Mexico (USA)** is one of the most fascinating destinations in the American Southwest. The cradle of 23 native tribes, it is the ideal place to immerse yourself in indigenous culture, its art, and its music under infinite skies.

The starting point is usually the historic city of **Santa Fe**, the state capital at the highest altitude in the USA, full of art galleries and museums. On your trip through the state, Bandelier National Monument, with its ancestral caves, and Taos, declared a World Heritage Site, await you. Don't forget to experience the hot air balloons in Albuquerque, an unforgettable visual spectacle.
