There are moments in travel that by themselves justify thousands of miles of journeying. Watching the sun rise behind the iconic towers of **Angkor Wat** in Cambodia is one of them. This temple, the largest religious monument in the world, transforms into a mystical stage as the first lights of day begin to silhouette its profile reflected in the lotus ponds.

Although you will share this moment with hundreds of other travelers, the reverent silence that takes over as the sky shifts from purple to orange hues is deeply moving. Angkor Wat is not just a feat of Khmer architecture; it is a place where time seems to stand still and spirituality can be felt in every carved stone. It is, without a doubt, the most spectacular awakening one can experience in the heart of the Cambodian jungle.
