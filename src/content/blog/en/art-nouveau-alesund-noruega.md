**Ålesund** is a city where mountains and fjords meet the ocean. Want to see how spectacular this landscape is? You just have to climb the 418 steps to the **Aksla** viewpoint and enjoy a panoramic view that will leave you speechless.

This Art Nouveau-style city is perfectly located among a group of islands reaching out into the Atlantic Ocean. Its reconstruction after a devastating fire in 1904 gave it its unique and elegant character, making it one of the most visited spots in the country. It is a fantastic starting point for exploring the fjord region and enjoying an active holiday in the heart of Norwegian nature.
