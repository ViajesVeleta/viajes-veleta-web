The Port of **Piraeus** in Athens is much more than a simple transport hub; it is the beating heart of navigation in Greece and the historical gateway to the Aegean Sea. This image captures the silhouette of a vessel resting under the Mediterranean sun, in a place that has witnessed the arrival and departure of sailors for millennia.

Walking through its docks is to be caught up in a spirit of constant adventure. From here, great ocean liners and ferries depart connecting the capital with the Greek islands, creating an atmosphere of bustle and possibility. Amidst the scent of salt air and the incessant movement of ships, Piraeus reminds us that Athens has always looked to the sea to find itself.
