The **Mediterranean** has the capacity to make us feel at home, even when we are in the middle of its deep waters. This photograph, taken from the cruise ship *MSC Fantasia*, captures that exact moment when the day says goodbye and the horizon is tinged with a luminous melancholy.

No great words are needed to describe what it feels like to watch the sun sink into our *Mare Nostrum*. It is a reminder of the light that has guided sailors for millennia and which today, for the modern traveler, remains a spectacle of overwhelming simplicity and power. A small visual tribute to our closest and most beloved sea.
