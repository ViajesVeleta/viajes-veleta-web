**The light of Malaga.**

The 'Farola' of the port of Malaga, a symbol of the city since 1817, was recently declared a Site of Cultural Interest. This iconic tower, designed by Joaquín Mª Pery y Guzmán, is one of the few lighthouses with a feminine name in Spain. Originally conceived only to support the lantern, it today overlooks a modern and vibrant port that maintains all its Mediterranean essence.

**Photography:** taken from the Alcazaba, providing a panoramic view of the port with the lighthouse as the undisputed protagonist of Malaga's coastal light.
