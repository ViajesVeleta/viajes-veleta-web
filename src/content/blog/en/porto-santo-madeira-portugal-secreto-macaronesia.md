**Porto Santo** is much in a small space. This small volcanic island in Macaronesia, chiseled by the wind and the Atlantic, is the \"holy port\" named by 15th-century Portuguese navigators. Located just a few hours from Madeira —recently voted Europe's best island destination— Porto Santo offers a sanctuary where the sand has therapeutic properties and the water is a crystal-clear embrace.

It hardly ever rains and the land resists cultivation, transforming this enclave into an \"open-air spa.\" Its miles-long beach is the main draw for those seeking to heal body and soul away from conventional tourist circuits. A well-kept secret in the middle of the ocean that invites contemplation and absolute rest among volcanic landscapes of austere and captivating beauty.
