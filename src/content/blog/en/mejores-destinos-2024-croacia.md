**Croatia** always appears on best destination lists: its sunny coasts, its more than 1,200 islands, and its walled cities full of history have everything to succeed. After 10 years in the European Union, its entry into the Schengen area and the adoption of the euro make visiting it in 2024 easier than ever.

Although the coast gets most of the attention, perhaps it's time to discover less crowded spots like the **Peljesac peninsula**, now famous for its spectacular bridge. And let's not forget the interior, with the almost supernatural beauty of the **Plitvice** lakes and the bucolic lands of Zagorje.
