**Charging Bull: The bronze heart that never sleeps**

If you visit **New York**, there is a mandatory stop in the **Financial District** that represents much more than a simple sculpture: the *Charging Bull*. This bronze colossus weighing over 3 tons, located in the historic **Bowling Green** plaza, is the ultimate symbol of the optimism and strength of the American market.

What many do not know is that its origin was an act of rebellion. The artist **Arturo Di Modica** installed it illegally in front of the Stock Exchange in 1989 as a Christmas gift after the financial crisis, seeking to inspire New Yorkers to "charge" against adversity. Today, it is one of the most photographed spots in the world.
