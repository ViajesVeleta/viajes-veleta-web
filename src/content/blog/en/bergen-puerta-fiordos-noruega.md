Founded in the 11th century by a Viking king, **Bergen** is the second-largest city in Norway and undoubtedly one of the most beautiful. Known as the gateway to the country's most spectacular fjords, Bergen is the perfect size to explore on foot.

The city preserves significant medieval monuments, avant-garde museums, and a privileged natural environment easily accessible by funicular or ferry. Although its latitude might suggest extreme cold, the Gulf Stream softens its climate, making summer the ideal season to get lost in its colorful streets and historic **Bryggen** wharf.
