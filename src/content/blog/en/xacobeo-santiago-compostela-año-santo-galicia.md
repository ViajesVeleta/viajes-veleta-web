Galicia is one of the most sought-after destinations this year thanks to the celebration of **Xacobeo**. But what exactly is it? The Holy Year or Jubilee Year is a time of grace that allows pilgrims to obtain a plenary indulgence after visiting the Apostle's tomb in the **Santiago Cathedral**.

Extraordinarily, this Xacobeo is celebrated during 2021 and 2022 due to the exceptional circumstances of the pandemic. Pope Francis confirmed this extension, keeping the Holy Door open for two consecutive years. It is a unique opportunity to walk the **Camino de Santiago** (Way of St. James) and experience the magic of arriving at the Plaza del Obradoiro in an atmosphere of spirituality and shared joy.
