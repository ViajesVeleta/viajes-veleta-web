They say that size matters, and at this gas station on **Route 66**, the owner seems to have taken that saying very seriously. His love for the American flag knows no bounds, nor contained dimensions.

It is extremely common to see the United States flag on all types of buildings, whether public or private. For Americans, it is a source of deep pride, and they do not hesitate to hoist it in the most unexpected places. Even in the most modest gardens, the most prominent spot is always reserved for a flagpole where the stars and stripes can wave for all to see. On the legendary *Mother Road*, these displays of patriotism become another element of the landscape, as iconic as the neon signs of motels or vintage gas pumps.
