We return to the blog with a post where expert **Paco Nadal** provides insights and reasons to visit Cuba. It appears the country's situation has improved since our trip last year.

*Post-covid, the Caribbean island needs tourism more than ever. Despite having a political and economic system that is more than open to criticism, it is a fascinating island, with friendly people full of life and incredible locations.*

Trinidad, with its cobblestone streets and colorful colonial architecture, is a perfect example of that Cuban magic that remains intact despite the challenges.
