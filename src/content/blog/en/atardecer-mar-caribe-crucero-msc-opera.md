There is no better way to end the year than contemplating the infinite horizon from the heart of the **Caribbean Sea**. This image was captured on the last day of 2017 from the deck of the cruise ship *MSC Opera*, as we sailed through warm, crystalline waters.

The Caribbean has a special light, a golden hue that seems to melt over the sea as the sun begins to set. It is the perfect moment to reflect on the miles traveled, the cultures discovered, and the photographs that have brought this blog to life over the last twelve months. With this sunset, we close one cycle and prepare for the new destinations awaiting us on the other side of the calendar. Happy New Year to all world travelers!
