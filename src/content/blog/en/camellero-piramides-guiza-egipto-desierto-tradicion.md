There is no image that better summarizes the mystique of eternal **Egypt** than the silhouette of a camel driver outlined against the imposing Pyramids of Giza. This scene, captured on the outskirts of Cairo, transports us to a world where time seems to have stood still for millennia.

Camel drivers are an inseparable part of the Giza Plateau landscape. With their knowledge of the terrain and their animals, they offer travelers the chance to traverse the desert the traditional way, feeling the majesty of the Great Pyramid of Khufu and its sisters from a unique perspective. It is a direct connection to the past, in a place where the sand and sky unite to guard one of humanity's greatest legacies.
