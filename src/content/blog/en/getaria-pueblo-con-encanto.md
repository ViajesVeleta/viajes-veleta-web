**Getaria** has been the birthplace of great historical figures: Juan Sebastián Elkano, author of the first circumnavigation of the world, and the legendary designer Cristóbal Balenciaga. His museum is today a mandatory stop for fashion enthusiasts.

But Getaria is much more than its illustrious residents. Its medieval village, its fishing port, and its natural surroundings make it one of the most charming towns on the Cantabrian coast. Furthermore, you cannot leave without trying **txakoli**, the local white wine with a designation of origin produced on its seaward slopes.
