This week I leave you with a destination recommended by "Viajar" magazine: **Costa Rica**.

**The country with the greatest diversity on the planet holds dozens of secrets that make it every traveler's dream.**

In Costa Rica, paths lead to waterfalls, crater lakes, deserted beaches surrounded by lush jungle, cloud forests, and impressive national parks. The "tico" country is the dream of every adventurer, outdoor lover, and jungle scenery enthusiast. It's no wonder, as its small borders hold places like the **Arenal Volcano**, with impressive routes like the ascent to **Cerro Chato** or its romantic thermal springs.

Also in its **Manuel Antonio National Park**, with palm trees swaying in the wind and an infinity of tropical birds. Costa Rica is **Tortuguero**, with its wide canals where you can paddle a canoe; **Sarapiquí**, with impressive eco-lodges, or **Montezuma**, perfect for lovers of artistic beach culture.

Tours and destinations throughout its green geography earned it the **VIAJAR Award for International Sustainable Destination**. But above all, the 'Pura Vida' country holds dozens of curiosities that you must know before immersing yourself in its passionate culture.
