At La Rábida, within the municipality of **Palos de la Frontera**, lies one of the most fascinating places for history lovers: the **Wharf of the Caravels**. Here rest the exact replicas of La Niña, La Pinta, and La Santa María, built in 1992 to commemorate the 5th Centenary of the Discovery of America.

Stepping on board these vessels allows you to imagine the hardship and courage of the sailors who in 1492 set sail into the unknown. You can descend into the holds or enter **Christopher Columbus's** cabin, the exact place where the courses to the New World were charted. The visit is completed with a recreated medieval neighborhood and the Encounter Island, showing the cultural clash of that historic voyage.
