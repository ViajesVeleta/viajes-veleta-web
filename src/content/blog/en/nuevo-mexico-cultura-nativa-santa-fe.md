New Mexico is, for many, the place where you can still experience \"authentic American culture.\" Birthplace of 23 native tribes, this Southwestern state of the **United States** offers a unique immersion into indigenous history through its art, music, and ancient traditions.

The ideal starting point is the historic city of **Santa Fe**, the highest state capital in the US. Its streets are lined with art galleries and specialized museums, such as the Museum of Contemporary Native Arts. During our **Route 66** trip, we visited iconic spots like the Yellow Horse Trading Post and the town of Taos, a World Heritage site. With its infinite skies and desert sunsets, New Mexico is a destination that connects travelers with the essence of the land.
