The island of **Terceira**, in the Azores archipelago, has established itself as one of the most desired destinations for those seeking a safe haven surrounded by nature. Its sparse population and exemplary management of health challenges make it an oasis of tranquility away from mass tourism.

One of its strong points is undoubtedly the vastness of its green landscapes. From **Serra do Cume**, you can behold a unique panoramic view of the island that looks like a natural patchwork quilt. Terceira invites you to disconnect under infinite skies and among meadows that merge with the Atlantic's blue, offering one of the most authentic and revitalizing experiences in Portugal.
