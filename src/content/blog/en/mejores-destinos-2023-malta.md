Malta remains a very special place to relax and have fun. In the heart of the Mediterranean, this small country brings together attractions ranging from its rich history and artistic heritage in **Valletta**, to its growing range of outdoor activities on the islands of **Gozo** and **Comino**.

Valletta is constantly transforming; its newest buildings, such as the Parliament designed by Renzo Piano, coexist with centuries-old walls. Although the famous Azure Window of Gozo disappeared in 2017, Dwejra Bay and the Inland Sea still offer spectacular landscapes that you cannot miss.
