Rubén Blades once stated: *"the only ones in a position to survive an apocalypse now are the Cubans; they have been in disaster for decades."*

The masterful Panamanian musician describes the current situation in Cuba with this stark sentence. However, this does not take away from the tourist interest in a destination that remains one of the most attractive in the Caribbean. It differs from others in that, besides having sun and paradisiacal beaches, it offers many other attractions, including its history, culture, climate, and architectural, landscape, and artistic heritage. If you want to know today's Cuba, hurry; change is approaching and feels unstoppable.
