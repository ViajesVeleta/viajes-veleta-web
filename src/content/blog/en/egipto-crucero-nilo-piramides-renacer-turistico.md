After several difficult years marked by instability, **Egypt** is experiencing a hopeful rebirth in tourism. More and more travelers are returning to classic tours that combine the mystique of Nile cruises—lasting four to seven days—with the fascinating energy of Cairo.

A visit to the **Giza Pyramids** remains the main attraction and an experience that never loses its magnetism. On the Giza plateau, it is a common sight to see camel drivers patiently waiting for tourists to offer a ride with the millennia-old pyramids in the background, a scene that seems frozen in time. The scheduling of direct charter flights from Spain has made access to this legendary destination easier and more affordable, allowing a new generation of travelers to discover the secrets of Pharaonic civilization.
