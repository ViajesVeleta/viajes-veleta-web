The **Aurresku** of honor is one of the deepest hallmarks of Basque cultural identity. It is a traditional dance performed as a tribute and mark of respect in acts of great social relevance, such as weddings, inaugurations, or official receptions.

While historically an exclusively male discipline, today it is common to see female dantzaris executing these steps full of strength and elegance. This week's images were captured in the city of **San Sebastián** during a wedding celebration. In them, we can see the intensity and virtuosity of the dantzaris as they dance before the couple, turning a private moment into a solemn ceremony of roots and tradition. San Sebastián, with its incomparable setting, grants this dance an atmosphere of distinction that leaves no spectator indifferent.
