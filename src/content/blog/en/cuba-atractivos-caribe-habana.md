Master musician Rubén Blades once said about the island's situation: *"the only ones in a condition to survive an apocalypse now are the Cubans; they have been in the disaster for decades"*. However, that resilience is part of the magic of a destination that remains one of the most attractive in the **Caribbean**.

Cuba differs from any other destination because of its depth: it offers living history, a vibrant culture, a colonial architectural heritage that resists time, and an unmatched artistic spirit. Beyond the postcard beaches, getting to know **today's Cuba** is to witness a world in transition. If you have the chance, visit soon; change seems unstoppable, and the soul of its neighborhoods, like those in **Havana**, is a treasure that deserves to be experienced.
