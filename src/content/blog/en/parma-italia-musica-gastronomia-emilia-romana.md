In our itinerary through the Italian region of **Emilia-Romagna**, Parma stands out as a destination that makes you fall in love instantly. With a noble past linked to the Farnese and Bourbon dynasties, this city is considered a \"Little Paris\" for its refined appearance and numerous monuments.

Parma is not only about culture, theater, and music —with the immortal **Giuseppe Verdi** as its musical symbol— but also the capital of the Italian *Food Valley*. Designated a UNESCO Creative City for Gastronomy, it is the origin of world-class products such as **Parma ham** and **Parmigiano Reggiano**. A magical place to get lost in its stories and its flavors, envied all over the world.
