I have chosen a photograph taken in **Kakadu National Park** to bid farewell to this year. It is a twilight image of a beautiful location that remains practically in its virgin natural state, a place to which the adjective **"singular"** can be assigned like few others in the world.

The photograph was captured during a boat excursion on **Yellow Water**, one of the most spectacular sites to be found within the park. As the boat glided silently through the billabongs, the sky turned shades of violet and orange, reflecting in waters where Australian wildlife prepares for the night. It is a moment of absolute peace that summarizes the essence of travel: discovering corners where humans are merely privileged spectators of the earth's grandeur.

We end this cycle with this light, grateful for the shared images and ready to discover other amazing places in the coming year.
