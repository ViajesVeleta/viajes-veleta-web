**Praia da Rocha**, located in the municipality of Portimão, is the quintessential tourist image of the **Algarve**. With a sandy beach stretching for 3 kilometers, this beach is famous for its unique rock formations sculpted by the sea over millennia.

It is one of the liveliest summer resorts in southern Portugal, ideal for those seeking relaxation in its calm waters as well as for lovers of nightlife and sports. During our trip in February, we were able to enjoy its vastness before the arrival of the summer crowds, confirming that it is an essential destination for any route through Portuguese lands.
