All over the world there are things you can do for free, but the federal capital of the United States takes the prize. Here you can enjoy yourself to the fullest without spending a dollar in its museums, parks, or guided tours of the **Capitol**.

Washington is a global political center and a tremendously dynamic city. Most of its museums, such as those of the Smithsonian Institution, are free and of amazing quality. As evening falls, life moves to lively neighborhoods like **U Street** or **Logan Circle**, perfect for grabbing a beer and enjoying the American night.
