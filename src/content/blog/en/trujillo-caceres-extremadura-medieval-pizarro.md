Forged through different eras and architectural visions, the Extremaduran city of **Trujillo** is the perfect destination for a road trip through the province of Cáceres. This enclave allows visitors to witness the evolution from a Roman settlement into one of the most important medieval towns in Spain.

A visit to its **Plaza Mayor** is essential, presided over by the iconic equestrian statue of **Francisco Pizarro**, an illustrious son of the city. From there, the climb towards the walls and the castle reveals a labyrinth of parish churches and manor houses that speak of a past linked to the discovery of America. A place where stone tells stories of conquerors and ancient kingdoms under the Extremaduran sun.
