The celebration of the International Tourism Trade Fair (**Fitur**) in Madrid, from May 19 to 23, 2021, was a key milestone for the sector. This event showcased various destinations in Spain ideal for a getaway—a perfect way to get back into the rhythm of traveling.

A standout example is **Malaga**, recognized as the European Capital of Smart Tourism through September 2021. The city shines for its accessibility, sustainability, and a cultural offering that includes the Picasso Museum. Enjoying an *espeto* (sardine skewer) at its port or taking in the views from Gibralfaro Castle are experiences that remind us why we love to travel.
