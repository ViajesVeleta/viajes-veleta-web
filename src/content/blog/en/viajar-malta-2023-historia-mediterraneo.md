Malta remains one of Europe's most special destinations. In the heart of the Mediterranean, this small archipelago manages to blend the ancient with the cutting-edge. Its capital, **Valletta**, brimming with artistic heritage, has reinvented itself with projects such as Renzo Piano's Parliament building.

Beyond its rich history, Malta invites adventure on islands like Gozo and Comino, perfect for kayaking and hiking. Although the famous Azure Window is no longer there, landscapes like Dwejra Bay and the \"Inland Sea\" continue to captivate travelers from all over the world. A perfect mix of km 0 gastronomy, sun, and culture that Lonely Planet ranks among the best places to discover this year.
