Located within the **Navajo Nation** reservation, **Antelope Canyon** is one of the most coveted destinations for photography enthusiasts worldwide. This "slot canyon" is not only a geological wonder but also a sacred site for its custodians, which is why entrance is prohibited without a local Navajo guide.

The danger of the location during storm season—when flash floods can turn the canyon into a death trap in minutes—is momentarily forgotten when faced with the beauty inside. This week's photo showcases the fantastic shapes revealed as light filters down from above and reflects off the winding sandstone walls. The curved forms and hues of orange and purple create a dreamlike atmosphere, turning every corner of the canyon into a natural work of art that shifts constantly with the sun's position.
