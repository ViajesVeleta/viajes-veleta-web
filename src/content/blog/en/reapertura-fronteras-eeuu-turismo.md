The news of the reopening of borders in the **United States** has triggered travel bookings in Spain. Our country is one of those showing the most interest in returning to American territory, concentrating traffic mainly on routes to **New York**, **Chicago**, and Miami.

According to data from the specialized magazine **Nexotour**, New York remains the preferred destination, although cities like Los Angeles and San Francisco maintain their appeal. With an average trip duration of 17 days, Spaniards are looking for both quick business getaways and long stays to immerse themselves back in American culture after the long closure.
