Missed flights due to poor time calculation, problems at security checkpoints, or delays in immigration are common inconveniences that can ruin the start of a trip. Here are the most frequent mistakes and how to avoid them:

1. **Not researching the airport**: Many cities have multiple airports. Make sure you know which one you're going to and how its terminals are distributed.
2. **Time calculation**: Arrive 2 hours early for domestic flights and 3 hours for international ones. Leave enough buffer for connections.
3. **Online check-in**: Always do it online to save time and avoid overbooking issues.
4. **Prohibited items**: Review hand luggage rules to avoid confiscations and unnecessary delays.
5. **Flight status**: Don't rely solely on screens; use real-time tracking apps like FlightRadar24.
6. **Immigration rules**: Inform yourself about what you can and cannot do or bring to your destination country.
7. **Unauthorized transport**: Always use official transport means or trusted apps to get to your hotel.
8. **Losing your cool**: Always maintain your composure with airport staff, even in complex situations.
