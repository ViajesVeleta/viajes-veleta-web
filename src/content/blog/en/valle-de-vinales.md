**Viñales Valley**

Surrounded by mountains, the fertile Viñales Valley is dotted with spectacular rocky outcrops known as mogotes. Its inhabitants continue to use traditional agricultural techniques, particularly for tobacco production. The richness of its cultural landscape is enhanced by the traditional architecture of its farms and villages, where the characteristics of a peculiarly rich multi-ethnic society still endure, illustrating the cultural development of the Caribbean islands in general and Cuba in particular.
