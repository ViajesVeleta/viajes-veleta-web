If there is a medieval space that stands out in **Barcelona**, it is the **Plaça del Rei**, in the heart of the Gothic Quarter. It is, without a doubt, one of the most charming and mystical corners of the Catalan capital, where time seems to have stopped among its stone walls.

In this square, you will find the **City History Museum** (MUHBA), which offers a fascinating retrospective view of the key urban, economic, and social moments that shaped Barcelona from Roman times to the present day.
