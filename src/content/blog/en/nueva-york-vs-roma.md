*“While Rome shows itself as a conglomerate composed of strata of Western history, New York contains the greatest diversity of elements that perhaps can be seen in our time. A whole amalgam of people and typologies, of social and economic strata. Layers that overlap in a smaller and more current space of time.”* **David Jimenez.**

New York is not just a city, it is a human experiment in constant evolution where the modern devours the old to create something always new.
