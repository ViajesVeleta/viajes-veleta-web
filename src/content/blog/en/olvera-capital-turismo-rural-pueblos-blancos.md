Perched atop a rocky outcrop and surrounded by seas of olive trees, **Olvera** stands proud as the 2021 Rural Tourism Capital. It is the first time a town in the south of the peninsula has earned this recognition, standing out for its authenticity and landscape beauty.

Olvera fascinates with its whitewashed houses, steep alleys filled with geraniums, and the imposing castle that dominates the skyline. Located on the **white villages route of Cádiz**, near the Grazalema Natural Park, this town is a must-stop for those seeking the essence of Andalusia along an itinerary of over 200 kilometers that winding through pines, oaks, and olive groves.
