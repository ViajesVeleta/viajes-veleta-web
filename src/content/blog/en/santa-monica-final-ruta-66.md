**Santa Monica** is Los Angeles' hippy-chic little sister and, for many, its saving grace. It's the place where real-life *Lebowskis* share space with Hollywood producers, while families enjoy its wide beaches and the legendary pier.

Santa Monica Pier doesn't just offer an iconic Ferris wheel and a roller coaster with spectacular views of the Pacific; it's also the official endpoint of the legendary **Route 66**. A fascinating place that marks the close of an epic journey across the heart of the United States, full of life, attractions, and that incomparable Californian vibe.
