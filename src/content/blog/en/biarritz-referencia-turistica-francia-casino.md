Located just 40 km from San Sebastián, the French city of **Biarritz** is one of our neighbor's primary tourist landmarks. What was originally a modest fishing village was radically transformed when **Eugénie de Montijo**, wife of Napoleon III and originally from Granada, fell irredeemably in love with its beaches.

As the nobility of the era enjoyed the high life, she commissioned a palace that sparked envy across European royalty, turning Biarritz overnight into the fashionable destination for economic elites. Today, the city preserves that aura of exclusivity, offering beautiful beaches and a wide range of leisure and relaxation activities. If you visit, don't miss its famous casino, an emblematic building that remains a powerful focal point and a symbol of the glamour still breathed by this jewel of the French Basque coast.
