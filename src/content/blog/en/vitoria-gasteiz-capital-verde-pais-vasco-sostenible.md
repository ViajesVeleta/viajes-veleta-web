**Vitoria-Gasteiz** is undoubtedly the least known of the **Basque Country's** capitals, but perhaps the one most designed for its people. Awarded the *Global Green City* title, the city is a global example of sustainability thanks to its green spirit. Walking through its streets means enjoying a friendly city where nature and urbanism coexist in perfect harmony.

Home to the Basque Government and the Ajuria Enea Palace, Vitoria stands out for more than just its ecology. Its cultural life is incredibly vibrant, hosting one of Europe's most prestigious jazz festivals every year. Additionally, the **Artium** museum places the city at the forefront of contemporary art. A capital to walk through, breathe in, and be surprised by its quality of life.
