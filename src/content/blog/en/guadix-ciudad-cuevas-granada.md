Sheltered by Sierra Nevada lies the region of **Guadix**, a mystical territory where the earth is hollowed out to create homes. With more than two thousand inhabited **troglodyte dwellings** in the city alone, Guadix boasts the highest concentration of this type of housing in all of Europe.

These bioclimatic structures, which maintain a constant temperature of about 18-20º C throughout the year, are perfectly integrated into a clayey landscape of reddish tones. Wandering through the cave neighborhoods is to discover an ancestral way of life that continues to beat strongly just 40 kilometers from the city of Granada.
