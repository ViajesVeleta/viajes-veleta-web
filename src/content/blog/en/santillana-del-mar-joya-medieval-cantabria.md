Located just a step away from Santander, **Santillana del Mar** is known as one of the most beautiful towns in Spain. Its history began in the year 870 with a monastery that in the 12th century would become the imposing **Collegiate Church of Santa Juliana**, the Romanesque heart of the town.

The village preserves its medieval layout with cobbled streets flanked by noble houses with coats of arms and Baroque palaces from the 16th and 17th centuries, many of them built by \"indianos\" who returned from America. Strolling through the square presided over by the Collegiate Church is to step back in time among wooden balconies overflowing with geraniums and family crests carved in stone.
