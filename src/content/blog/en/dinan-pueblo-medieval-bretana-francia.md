**Dinan** is undoubtedly one of the jewels of **French Brittany**. This medieval town was a vital commercial enclave thanks to its strategic port on the Rance River. During our trip in August 2019, we were able to see why it is a must-visit for any traveler.

Its cobbled streets still preserve the spirit of the 14th and 18th centuries, when they overflowed with weavers, tanners, and carpenters. But Dinan does not only stand out for its craftsmanship; it played a crucial role in Breton history. From the defenses of the hero **Bertrand Du Guesclin** to the retreat of Queen **Anne of Brittany** in its majestic castle that overlooks the town, Dinan breathes history at every corner.
