Whether it's your first time or your sixth visit to **New York**, there is always something new to discover in this metropolis in constant construction and change. Its skyline grows at the pace of architectural innovations, while local customs evolve with current trends. Its resilience is extraordinary, having adapted to all adversities with inexhaustible energy.

The great advantage of New York is that it can be explored on foot, especially the island-district of **Manhattan**, which New Yorkers simply call *The City*. Walking itineraries, combined with a subway network that has substantially improved —thanks to new technologies and accessible stations— allow you to immerse yourself in its essence. Additionally, cycling has become a highly recommended option thanks to miles of exclusive lanes. Get ready to feel the pulse of the city that never sleeps!
