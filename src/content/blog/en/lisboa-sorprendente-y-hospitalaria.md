If you dream of your next trip to Europe, come and meet **Lisbon**, the historic city with a thousand stories to tell. A capital where the sun shines almost three hundred days a year and where one feels safe walking through its old neighborhoods both day and night.

Lisbon is authenticity: it's finding someone who explains with gestures the best place to go listen to **Fado**, it's tasting cod in any of its thousand ways, and it's letting yourself be carried away by its familiar hospitality. A city where ancient customs meet cultural innovation. Lisbon is ageless, but it loves company.
