When visiting the ancient Buddhist temples of **Sri Lanka**, it is common to encounter some very peculiar residents: the primates known as **\"Temple Monkeys\"**. These animals have made the archaeological zones their natural habitat and often accompany tourists, keeping an eye out for any slip-up that might allow them to get some food.

They are expert pickpockets and their boldness knows no bounds. One of their most unique features is their peculiar fringe, a hairstyle that inevitably brings Elvis Presley to mind. During our visit to **Sigiriya** and **Dambulla**, we could see them moving with total agility across cliffs and ruins, proving that in these sacred places, they are the true masters of the domain.
