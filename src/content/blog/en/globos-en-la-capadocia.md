**Balloons over Cappadocia**

One of the most unique and unforgettable experiences in the world is flying in a hot air balloon over the fascinating landscape of Cappadocia. The fairy chimneys, deep valleys, and eroded rock formations create an otherworldly landscape that is best appreciated from above as the sun begins to rise.

*The sky fills with hundreds of colorful balloons, creating a spectacle as impressive from the air as it is from the ground. It is a moment of silence and beauty that defines any trip to Turkey.*

**Photograph:** taken early in the morning from one of the viewpoints where the balloons launch.
