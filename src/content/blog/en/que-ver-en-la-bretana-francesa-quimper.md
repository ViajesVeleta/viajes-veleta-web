**Quimper: The Soul of Brittany**

Quimper is often described as the most "Breton" of all cities in French Brittany. With its twin-towered cathedral reaching for the sky and its river Odet winding through the center, it offers a perfect blend of history, art, and traditional culture.

*The old town is a delight for walkers. The half-timbered houses, some leaning at impossible angles, are painted in bright colors and house boutiques selling the famous Quimper pottery. Don't forget to visit the Saint-Corentin Cathedral, a masterpiece of Breton Gothic where the nave is curiously tilted to stay within the original foundations.*
