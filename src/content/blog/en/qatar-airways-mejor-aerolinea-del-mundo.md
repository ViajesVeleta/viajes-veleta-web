**Qatar Airways, a decade of success**

This is the eighth time since the creation of the ranking in 1999 that the Qatari airline, which has earned a solid reputation over the last decade, has taken the global crown. It had previously achieved this in 2011, 2012, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2021, and 2022.

The company, based at Hamad Airport in Doha—crowned as **the best airfield on the planet in 2024**—flies to 170 destinations and has a fleet of 230 aircraft, including ten A380s, the world's largest passenger airplane.
