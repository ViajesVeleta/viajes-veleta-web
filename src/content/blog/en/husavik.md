**The Diamond Circle: Northern Iceland**

Halfway along the route is the port town of **Húsavík**, where whale-watching boats outnumber all other vessels. Húsavík is for some the whale-watching capital of Iceland —if not of Europe— with baleen whales, dolphins, and even blue whales swimming through the wide Skjálfandi Bay.
