**Barcelona**

**Gothic Quarter**

Located in the Ciutat Vella district, in the heart of the city, the Gothic Quarter is the oldest nucleus and one of the most beautiful areas of Barcelona. The pleasant neighborhood is bounded by Las Ramblas, Vía Laietana, Paseo de Colón and Plaza de Cataluña.

Born on the remains of the Roman Barcino, medieval Barcelona favored the construction of Gothic churches and palaces that caused the disappearance of most of the Romanesque remains. The narrow and labyrinthine medieval streets of the Gothic Quarter of Barcelona make up the ideal setting to enjoy the city center without rushing.

**What to see in the Gothic Quarter**

The Gothic Quarter of Barcelona has numerous tourist attractions for all tastes, among which we could highlight:
Cathedral of Santa Eulalia, Plaza Sant Jaume, Carrer del Bisbe, Plaza Real, Plaza del Rey, Calle Portal de L'Angel, and Plaza Nova.
