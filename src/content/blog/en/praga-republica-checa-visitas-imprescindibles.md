There are a thousand and one reasons to visit **Prague**. Known as one of the most beautiful cities on the Old Continent, the capital of the Czech Republic attracts travelers from all over the world who fall in love with it. Autumn is, without a doubt, the ideal time to wander peacefully through its narrow streets.

From crossing the historic **Charles Bridge** to getting lost in the depths of its impressive castle, Prague feels like something out of a fairy tale. Its historical center, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, bears witness to a past marked by being the capital of the Kingdom of Bohemia and its rich Christian and Jewish traditions. All this accompanied, of course, by the taste of a good *pivo* by the Vltava River.
