Today I leave you with an image of one of the Loire castles visited on our group trip through French Brittany, the most spectacular of them all: **Chambord**. Also the link to the **France Tourism** website in case you want to know some more.

**Château de Chambord**

Less than 2 hours south of Paris, **Chambord** is an unmissable stop. It is the vastest and most prestigious of the French Renaissance castles. Through this majestic stone colossus, you will be able to soak up all the art of living of Francis I.
