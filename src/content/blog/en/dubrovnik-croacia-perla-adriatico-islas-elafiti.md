Known as the "Pearl of the Adriatic," the Croatian city of **Dubrovnik** is one of the premier Mediterranean destinations for travelers seeking history and heritage. This beautiful walled town offers visitors a legacy forged by the free spirit of its citizens, summarized in its motto: *"freedom is not sold for all the gold in the world."*

Walking along its massive walls allows you to gaze at the deep blue of the Adriatic while traveling back to the Middle Ages. But Dubrovnik is also a place of cultural coexistence, as seen in the mosque, synagogue, and Orthodox and Catholic temples that share space within its stone walls. Beyond the Old City, the nearby **Elafiti Islands** provide the perfect complement for an unforgettable getaway. Whether to revisit the filming locations of *Game of Thrones* or simply to enjoy its unbeatable climate, Dubrovnik is a mandatory stop that every traveler should cross off their list.
