**Bicycles are for summer (too), by Berna González Harbour**

There are two ways to travel the world: with the cultural drive to see every museum and cathedral, or with the drive to fly from one place to another taking it all in. For those of us who prefer the second option, the bicycle is the perfect excuse.

The speed of a bike is best for capturing the scents, sights, and impressions of an area without having to stop at every cobblestone. Walking is too slow, and a car puts you in a bubble of hurry. The bike is the ideal middle ground that allows you to see places without suffering through them and always escape at just the right moment.
