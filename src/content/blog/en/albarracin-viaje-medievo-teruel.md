Everything is stone in **Albarracín**, a monochromatic and spectacular gem nestled in the mountains of Teruel. Voted the most beautiful village in Spain by the readers of 'El Viajero' in 2018, its harmonic stones hold a fascinating story that invites an evocative journey to the Middle Ages.

Strolling through its streets means discovering reddish-toned houses, iron knockers on the doors, lace curtains, and carved wooden balconies. But its most attractive feature is its spectacular location, tucked into a bend of the **Guadalaviar River**. To truly enjoy the setting, nothing beats walking along the river path that embraces the village or climbing the ancient walls; the view of the town from above is well worth the effort.
