Located in the La Mancha region of Toledo, **Consuegra** is a town of Roman origin with an iconic horizon. At the foot of Cerro Calderico stand its twelve famous **windmills**, five of which still preserve their complete mechanism. Among them stands out the one named \"Sancho,\" in honor of Cervantes' masterpiece.

Next to the windmills, an impressive **castle** from the Reconquista era presides over the landscape, with a double line of walls and an imposing keep. But Consuegra holds more treasures: its Renaissance town hall, the Casa de la Tercia, and its squares with wooden corridors invite you to lose yourself in the soul of Castile. It was one of the unforgettable destinations of our last group trip.
