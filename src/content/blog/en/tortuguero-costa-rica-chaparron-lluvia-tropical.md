In the heart of the tropical rainforest, rain isn't an inconvenience—it's a blessing that brings life to everything surrounding you. During our visit to **Tortuguero National Park** in Costa Rica, we had the chance to "weather the storm," an experience that directly connects you with the power of nature.

This week's photograph captures that moment of forced pause when the skies open and water falls with an intensity only found at these latitudes. The sound of droplets hitting the giant leaves of the jungle and the scent of damp earth create a hypnotic atmosphere. In Tortuguero, where waterways replace roads, watching the landscape transform under the rain is an essential part of the adventure in this paradise of biodiversity.
