**Rochefort-en-Terre** is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful "Small Towns of Character" in **Brittany**. Set on a rocky outcrop, this village feels like a gem set in the heart of Morbihan, where every detail is meticulously cared for.

Its cobblestone streets, medieval houses adorned with flowers of a thousand colors, and artists' studios invite you on a true journey through time. In summer, the village becomes pedestrianized, allowing you to enjoy the charming Place du Puits and the artistic atmosphere that breathes from every corner with total happiness.
