The Norwegian city of **Bergen** is known worldwide as the gateway to the fjords, but it has a personality of its own that makes it one of Scandinavia's most beautiful and interesting destinations. This image captures the historic buildings of its port, a set of wooden facades that transport us directly to the commercial past of the Hanseatic League.

Its port is a permanent meeting place for both locals and travelers. Among its numerous bars and restaurants, it's easy to be caught up in the relaxed and cheerful atmosphere of a city that lives facing the sea. Getting lost in the narrow alleys branching off from the **Bryggen** wharf, declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is to discover the essence of Norway: a perfect blend of traditional architecture, maritime history, and the gateway to a wild and dazzling nature.
