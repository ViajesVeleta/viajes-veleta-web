Located within the Tierra del Fuego National Park, **Lapataia Bay** represents the final point of National Route 3 and, symbolically, the end of the road for many travelers journeying through the American continent. It is a fjord of icy waters and breathtaking landscapes where nature shows itself in its purest state.

The tranquility felt in this corner of the world is hard to describe. Surrounded by forests of lengas and guindos, and under the flight of caranchos and cauquenes, the bay gives us a picture of infinite calm. It is the perfect place to reflect on the journey traveled and let yourself be enveloped by the mysticism of the "end of the world."
