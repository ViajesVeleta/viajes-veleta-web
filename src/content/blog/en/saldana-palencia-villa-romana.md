**Saldaña**, in the Palencia region of Saldaña-Valdavia, is an essential stop for history lovers. Within its typically Castilian urban fabric, the church of San Pedro stands out, currently serving as the headquarters for the Monographic Museum of the **Roman Villa of La Olmeda**.

This archaeological site, located in Pedrosa de la Vega, preserves in situ an impressive collection of mosaics from the 3rd century AD. Walking through this ancient estate allows you to admire the grandeur of Rome through its detailed figurative and plant pavements, as well as to learn about its baths and necropolis.
