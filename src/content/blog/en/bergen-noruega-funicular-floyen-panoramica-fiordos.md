Founded in the 11th century by a Viking king, **Bergen** is Norway's second-largest city and undoubtedly one of the most beautiful in the entire country. This image, captured from above, shows the spectacular layout of a city that serves as the port of embarkation for the world's most impressive fjords.

With a friendly size that invites exploring its urban core on foot, Bergen preserves medieval monuments, avant-garde museums, and stunning natural landscapes in its surroundings. While its latitude might suggest extreme cold, the Gulf Stream softens its climate, making it an enjoyable destination year-round, though it shines with special intensity in summer. Whether taking the funicular up Mount **Fløyen** to behold this panorama or strolling through its historic streets, Bergen is a delight that every traveler should experience at least once in their lifetime.
