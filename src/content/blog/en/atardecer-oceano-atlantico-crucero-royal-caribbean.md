Sailing the **Atlantic Ocean** offers moments of absolute serenity found only when land lies far below the horizon. One of those magical moments is the arrival of twilight.

This image, captured from the deck of Royal Caribbean's *Adventure of the Seas*, shows the palette of warm colors the sun paints over the waters before vanishing. The gentle sway of the cruise ship and the rhythmic sound of the bow breaking the waves are the perfect accompaniment to a ritual that repeats every afternoon, yet in the middle of the ocean, takes on a much vaster and deeper dimension. It's a time to pause, breathe in the salt air, and let the vastness of the horizon envelop you.
