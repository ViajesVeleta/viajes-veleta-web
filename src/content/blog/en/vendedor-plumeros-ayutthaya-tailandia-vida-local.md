Located north of Bangkok, **Ayutthaya** is world-famous for its temple ruins, which once served as the capital of the historical Kingdom of Siam. However, in its modern streets, life continues to flow with a vitality and color that rival the grandeur of its past.

In this week's image, we encounter a **feather duster vendor** traversing the city offering his colorful merchandise. This contrast between history etched in stone and the simplicity of daily trades is what makes Thailand an inexhaustible destination. The dusters, with their vibrant hues, seem like a flash of joy amidst the asphalt, reminding us that beauty also resides in the most unexpected and traditional details of local life.
