Beyond its historical cities, **Peru** hides breathtaking landscape beauty in its highest peaks: the **nevados**. These giants of the Andes, covered in eternal and freezing white, are examples of nature in its maximum splendor that captivate everyone who ventures into their domains.

Spread across regions such as Áncash, Cusco, and Arequipa, these imposing volcanoes and mountains offer some of the most beautiful panoramas in the country. During our journey along the \"Sun Route,\" we were able to admire the grandeur of these ice-capped summits that crown the Andean horizon and are, undoubtedly, one of the lesser-known but most striking gems of Peruvian geography.
