**Getaria** is one of the most charming towns on the Cantabrian coast and a mandatory stop on any route through the **Basque Country**. The cradle of historical figures such as Juan Sebastián Elcano—the first man to circumnavigate the globe—and legendary designer Cristóbal Balenciaga, the town breathes pride and tradition in every one of its medieval streets.

In addition to visiting the Balenciaga Museum, which pays tribute to the master of haute couture, strolling through its old town and fishing port is a delight for the senses. Getaria is also synonymous with excellent dining; its grilled fish is celebrated, always paired with a glass of **Txakoli**, the white wine with designation of origin produced in the vineyards surrounding the town. With its views of the "Mouse of Getaria" (San Salvador Hill) and its maritime atmosphere, this Gipuzkoan town wins over everyone who visits.
