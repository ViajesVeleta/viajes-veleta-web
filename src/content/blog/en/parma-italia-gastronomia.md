**Parma** is a small Italian city with a noble past that captures you instantly. Considered a "little Paris" for its refined appearance, this gem of Emilia-Romagna is world-famous for being the home of composer Giuseppe Verdi.

But Parma is also the capital of the **Food Valley**. It is the source of products of excellence recognized across the globe: Parma Ham and Parmigiano Reggiano cheese. Walking through its streets is discovering a perfect blend of artistic beauty, theatrical history, and enviable flavors.
