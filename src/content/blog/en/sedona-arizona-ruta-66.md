Near Flagstaff, **Sedona** is a stop that leaves any traveler in awe. Its main attraction is the spectacular hiking trails that wind between rock formations with shades ranging from deep brown to the most intense red.

But Sedona is also known for its energy **vortexes**, such as *Airport Mesa*, places that many consider sources of positive and spiritual energy. Whether dusted in white by snow in winter or bathed in the golden light of spring, Sedona offers an unstoppable terrain and a natural beauty that seems out of this world.
