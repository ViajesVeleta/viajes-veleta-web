The **Blue Swallow Motel** is one of the most legendary and sought-after lodgings along the entire **Route 66**. Located in Tucumcari, New Mexico, this motel embodies the golden age of American road trips.

Construction began in 1939, and it reached its peak fame under the management of Lillian Redman, who received it as an engagement gift and ran it for four decades. Today, thanks to careful restoration, the Blue Swallow displays its famous original neon sign and offers rooms with adjacent garages, maintaining an unmistakable vintage charm. Tucumcari remains one of the most authentic stops on the "Main Street of America," where glowing signs and classic iconography transport you directly back to the 1950s.
