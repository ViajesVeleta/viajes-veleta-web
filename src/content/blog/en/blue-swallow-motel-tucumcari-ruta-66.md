The **Blue Swallow Motel** is one of the most legendary accommodations on the entire **Route 66**. Located in the authentic town of Tucumcari, New Mexico, this motel evokes the golden age of American road trips.

Opened in 1941, the motel was owned by Floyd and Lillian Redman for four decades, becoming a symbol of hospitality on the \"Mother Road.\" After a careful restoration, today it proudly displays its famous neon—one of the most original on the route—and rooms with vintage furniture and individual garages. **Tucumcari** remains one of the most genuine stops, preserving the glowing signs and iconography that made Route 66 the Main Street of America.
