Located in the emblematic Plaza de San Juan de Dios, the **Cádiz City Hall** is one of the most beautiful and representative civil buildings in the city. Its facade, in a marked Neoclassical and Elizabethan style, stands as a testament to the prosperity and history of the "Silver Cup."

The image captures the elegance of its columns and the harmony of its construction, which dominates the nerve center of local life. Walking through this square, with the City Hall in the background and the scent of salt air drifting in from the nearby port, is to immerse oneself in the essence of Cádiz: an open, luminous city steeped in millennia of history reflected in every stone. An essential stop to begin discovering the charms of Andalusia's most Atlantic capital.
