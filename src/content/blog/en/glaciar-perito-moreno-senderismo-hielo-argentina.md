Visiting the **Perito Moreno Glacier** in Argentine Patagonia is one of those experiences that stays with you forever. But if you want to take it a step further, hiking on the ice is the ultimate adventure. It all begins at Puerto Bajo Las Sombras, crossing Lake Rico to the southern face of this white giant.

Once there, equipped with crampons and guided by experts, we embark on a walk over the rough surface of the glacier. As you progress, you discover a world of cracks, sinkholes, and lagoons of intense blue. It is a silent and majestic journey that culminates in returning through the lush Andean Patagonian forest. An essential visit to feel the force of nature at the end of the world.
