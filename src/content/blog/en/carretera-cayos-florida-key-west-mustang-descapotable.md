Located about 350 km from Miami, **Key West** is the final destination of one of the most unique and beautiful roads on the planet. The magnificent climate Florida enjoys most of the year makes this route a top-tier tourist resource.

The road sometimes travels over stretches of land so narrow there is no room even for power lines, which must be situated over the sea. In many sections, the asphalt directly flies over the turquoise waters of the Caribbean and the Atlantic. If you travel to Miami, this excursion is almost mandatory. My personal advice: beg for a convertible at the rental office; if you're lucky enough to get a Mustang, I assure you that crossing these bridges over the ocean will be an experience you'll never forget.
