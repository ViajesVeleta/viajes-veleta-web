Starting in the spring of 2024, **Venice** will become the first city in the world to charge a **tourist entry fee**. Day visitors who do not stay overnight in the city must pay a five-euro fee to access the capital of Veneto.

This experimental measure seeks to discourage mass tourism that threatens the city's integrity. Recently included by UNESCO on its list of **heritage in danger**, Venice is fighting against climate change and the social and architectural wear and tear of receiving millions of people every year. A necessary effort to preserve the cultural identity of one of the most beautiful cities on the planet.
