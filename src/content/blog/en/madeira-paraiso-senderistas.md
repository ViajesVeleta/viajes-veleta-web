**Madeira, the tropical pearl of the Atlantic, is a paradise for hikers without vertigo.**

This small volcanic island offers exceptional landscapes. The **Vereda do Areeiro** is one of the most attractive trails, traveling along the peaks above seas of clouds. Another essential hike is the **Levada do Rei**, which introduces the traveler to the laurisilva, the primitive forest that covered Europe millions of years ago.

In **Funchal**, its friendly capital, you cannot miss the Mercado de Lavradores and its colorful exotic fruits. And for a unique experience, descend from the Monte in the typical **carros de cesto**, large wicker baskets that slide down the slopes on wooden rails.
