The reopening of **United States** borders has marked a turning point for international tourism, triggering a surge in bookings from Spain. Our country leads the interest in traveling to American territory, with traffic mainly concentrated on routes to New York, Chicago, and Miami.

While **New York** remains the clear favorite, cities like **Chicago** are regaining their rhythm with stays that show a balance between short-term business trips and longer tourist getaways. With an average trip duration of 17 days, Spaniards are once again discovering the plazas and skyscrapers of metropolises like the "Windy City," confirming the pent-up desire to cross the Atlantic once more.
