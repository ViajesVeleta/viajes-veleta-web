Few moments in a trip are as magical as witnessing the **sunset at Angkor Wat**. In this image, we capture the instant when the sun begins its descent, bathing the waters of the canal surrounding Cambodia's most famous temple in a warm, coppery light.

Beyond its defensive or ornamental function, the Angkor Wat canal acts as a giant mirror where sky and earth merge. In this corner of **Siem Reap**, time seems to stand still as the reflection of the towers softly blurs on the water's surface. It is the perfect end to a day of exploration among millennial stones and jungle, a reminder of the peace that emanates from this sanctuary as the day draws to a close.
