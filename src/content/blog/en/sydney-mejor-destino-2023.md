**Sydney** tops the lists for 2023 as the perfect destination to reconnect after years of isolation. The capital of New South Wales boasts an inclusive and vibrant character, inviting travelers to rediscover its icons from a new perspective.

Strolling through **Circular Quay** to see the majestic **Sydney Opera House** —which celebrates its 50th anniversary— is just the beginning. From the historic botanical gardens to the legendary sands of **Bondi Beach** or **Manly**, Sydney combines unique artistic and gastronomic diversity with urban nature that continues to place it among the most surprising cities on the planet.
