Located next to the mouth of the Arade River in **Portimão**, **Praia da Rocha** boasts a vast 3 km sandy beach dotted with curious rock formations sculpted by the sea over millennia.

It is, without a doubt, the quintessential tourist image of the **Algarve**. Its calm and warm waters invite for a swim, while its lively nightlife and Casino make it one of the most vibrant summer centers in southern Portugal. A destination where sun and sport meet on the shores of the Atlantic.
