**Tortuguero: Nature and Color**

Tortuguero is one of those places that feels like it belongs to another world. Small, colorful, and accessible only by boat or plane, this village in the heart of the Costa Rican jungle is a sanctuary for wildlife and a lesson in living in harmony with nature.

*The sound of the jungle is the soundtrack here. Between the canals, you can spot monkeys, sloths, and hundreds of bird species. But the real magic happens on the beach, where green turtles return every year to nest, a spectacle of life that has turned this village into a global icon of conservation.*
