The unusual tranquility that reigned in many cities worldwide a few years ago led to scenes that seemed straight out of a fable. With streets emptied of people and noise, animals of all kinds ventured out to explore territory usually reserved for humans.

In the city of **Adelaide**, South Australia, we came across this curious sounder of pigs wandering freely along the sidewalks. As seen in the photo, they moved with surprising ease, as if they had decided the urban space was theirs by right. These images reminded us of nature's capacity to reclaim its place as soon as the human bustle fades, gifting us moments of surprise and reflection on our coexistence with the animal kingdom.
