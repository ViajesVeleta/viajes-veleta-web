**The Twelve Apostles (State of Victoria, Australia)**

The **Great Ocean Road** is one of Australia's most famous road routes. It passes by world-class surf breaks, rainforests, quiet coastal towns, and trees full of koalas. It winds through impressive limestone cliffs and allows you to feel the power of the ocean up close.

Its main landmark is the **Twelve Apostles**, majestic rock formations emerging from the sea that are the most iconic image of the State of Victoria. But beyond the photo, the best part is the journey: driving slowly through the curves of the Bass Strait and discovering treasures like Port Fairy or Cape Bridgewater.
