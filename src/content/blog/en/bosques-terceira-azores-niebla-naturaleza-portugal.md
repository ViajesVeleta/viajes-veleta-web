**Terceira** Island, in the Azores archipelago, is a paradise of greenery and untamed nature. This photograph was taken in the forests surrounding **Lagoa dos Patos**, one of the island's lushest and most magical enclaves.

In winter, it is very common for dense mists to cover much of the territory, transforming the landscape into a setting that seems straight out of an ancient legend. The humidity and the silence, broken only by the dripping of water on the leaf litter, create an atmosphere of mystery and absolute peace. Walking through these paths under the white mantle of the fog is to connect with the purest essence of the Azores, where the land and sea merge in a moist, green embrace.
