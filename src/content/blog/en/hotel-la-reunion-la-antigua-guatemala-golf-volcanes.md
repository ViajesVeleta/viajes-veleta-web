Located just fifteen minutes from the historic city of La Antigua, the **La Reunión Hotel** represents one of the peaks of luxury and exclusivity in Guatemala. This image of its golf course shows the primary reason for its fame: a privileged location surrounded by overwhelming nature.

The exquisite quality of its services is matched by a unique geographical setting. Playing a round of golf here means doing so under the watchful eye of the imposing volcanic peaks that dominate the horizon. The combination of pristine greens, pure mountain air, and those spectacular views makes any stay at this hotel an unforgettable experience. It is, undoubtedly, a sanctuary of peace and beauty for those seeking to disconnect in a setting where man and volcano seem to coexist in perfect balance.
