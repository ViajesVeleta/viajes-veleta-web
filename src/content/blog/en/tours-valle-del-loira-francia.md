**Tours** is a city that breathes vitality. Sitting on the banks of the Loire, this historical capital of France under Louis XI combines Renaissance splendor with the dynamism of a young and open university city.

Its old town, a World Heritage Site, is centered around the **Saint Martin** district and lively squares like **Plumereau**, surrounded by half-timbered houses. But Tours is also the birthplace of writer **Honoré de Balzac** and home to one of the most beautiful Gothic cathedrals in France. Among century-old gardens and modern architecture by Victor Laloux, Tours is the perfect starting point to explore the magic of the Loire castles.
