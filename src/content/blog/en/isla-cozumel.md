**A destination of adventure and relaxation**

Cozumel is the largest island in the Mexican Caribbean and the third largest in Mexico. Rich in history, Maya vestiges, and natural wonders; an authentic paradise in the middle of the sea with white beaches and beautiful sunsets. World-famous for its coral reefs, it is an adventure and relaxation destination with all kinds of activities in impressive natural settings.

Located off the coast of Playa del Carmen, the island is approximately 48 kilometers long and 16 kilometers wide. Formerly known as Kuzamil, which means "the island of the swallows" in Maya, it is today a favorite destination for cruisers and divers. Cozumel is recognized as one of the top five diving spots in the world, with coral barriers that reach exceptional depths.
