The **snow-capped peaks** (nevados) are one of the most spectacular and least known attractions of Peru. With extraordinary white covering their highest summits, these ice masses are a scenic beauty that enchants anyone who gazes upon them.

Peru has various peaks in all their splendor, mainly in the regions of Ancash, Huanuco, Lima, Arequipa, and Cusco. A visit to the Peruvian Andes is all that is needed to discover the grandeur and silence of these summits that hold centuries of natural history.
