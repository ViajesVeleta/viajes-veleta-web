The park offers an extensive network of walkways and trails, such as the **Upper Circuit**, the **Lower Circuit**, and the **Macuco Trail**, allowing visitors a total **immersion experience** in nature, observing the falls from multiple angles and experiencing the ecosystem's energy.

**Photograph**: taken with an analog camera in 2012 and later scanned, showing one of the walkways located in **Foz do Iguaçu** (Brazil), where you can see the **Garganta del Diablo**.
