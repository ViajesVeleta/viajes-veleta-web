**Chicago**, the third-largest city in the United States, is world-renowned for its cutting-edge architecture and legendary skyscrapers. Currently, the city's silhouette features over 85 buildings rising above 160 meters, creating one of the most impressive horizons in the world.

The undisputed protagonist remains the **Willis Tower** (formerly Sears Tower), which at 108 stories and 442 meters, was the world's tallest building for 25 years. Of the ten tallest buildings in the United States, four are located here in the "Windy City." Witnessing this landscape of steel and glass at sunset, from the **Navy Pier** recreation area beside the vast waters of Lake Michigan, is an experience that truly defines the essence of the American metropolis.
