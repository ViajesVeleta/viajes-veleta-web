The legendary Buddha head entwined within the roots of a tree in **Ayutthaya** is one of Thailand's most iconic images. Legend has it that a pious farmer found it on the ground and, horrified by the disrespect toward the sacred, carefully placed it upon the roots of a young tree. Over the centuries, the tree grew, embracing the stone and creating this image of harmony between nature and the spiritual.

For Buddhists, this is no coincidence but a display of Buddha's power and serene wisdom. Those seeking a more rational explanation will see an astounding whim of nature, but it is impossible to deny the visual poetry emanating from this corner. Whatever the interpretation, the **Enlightened One** watches us from his vegetal throne with a peace that survives the passage of time and human discourse.
