Known as the **Golden Island**, Porto Santo is the small and unknown sister of Madeira. Unlike its older sister, this island is arid, flat, and possesses an infinite beach of golden sand with therapeutic properties that make it a treasure in the middle of the Atlantic.

Porto Santo is sun, sand, and sea in its purest state. A volcanic territory where beauty lies in its enigma and austerity. It preserves a genuine character that seems to date back to the dawn of time, a microworld of just 43 km² that is an essential visit for those seeking peace and virgin landscapes.
