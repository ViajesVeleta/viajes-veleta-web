Located between the majestic Colca Canyon and the city of Arequipa, the **Bofedales de Toqra** are a fascinating ecosystem and a privileged spot for observing Andean wildlife. These moist and fertile wetlands are home to three of Peru's most iconic animals: **alpacas, llamas, and vicuñas**.

In addition to these camelids, the wetlands attract a wide variety of high Andean birds, most notably wild ducks and flamingos. Local myths place the origin of alpacas in these serene and beautiful landscapes, a belief that is easy to understand when witnessing the harmony and life that emanate from these lands.
