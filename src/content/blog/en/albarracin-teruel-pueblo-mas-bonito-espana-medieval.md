Everything is stone in **Albarracín**, a monochromatic gem rising spectacularly amidst the mountain ranges of Teruel. Often voted the most beautiful village in Spain, its streets provide a harmonious stage that invites an evocative journey to the heart of the Middle Ages.

The most fascinating aspect of Albarracín is its appearance: reddish-hued houses, artistic door knockers, lace curtains, and balconies of iron and carved wood that seem frozen in time. Yet its greatest appeal lies in its strategic location, carved into a gorge of the Guadalaviar River. To capture all its magic, there's nothing like walking the riverside path that hugs the meander or climbing its imposing walls to behold one of the most stunning urban views on the peninsula. It is a place where silence and stone whisper stories of centuries past.
