To really understand Mexico and Mexicans, there's nothing like the rancheras of **José Alfredo**. Music is a fundamental resource for understanding and appreciating the soul of this country.

In addition to the classics, we recommend listening to wonderful contemporary artists like **Lila Downs** and **Natalia Lafourcade**, whose voices capture Mexican tradition and modernity. Mexico enters through the ears and stays in the heart forever.
