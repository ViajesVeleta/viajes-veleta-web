The city of **Bethlehem**, in Palestine, is a place where time seems to have stood still among its cobblestone streets and ocher-toned buildings. This image offers a perspective of the city, a corner of the world whose name resonates in the history and memory of millions.

Near the center stands the **Church of the Nativity**, the place tradition identifies as the exact spot where Jesus of Nazareth was born. Beyond its religious importance, Bethlehem is a vibrant city with a very distinct identity, where the hospitality of its people and the beauty of its setting in the Judean hills create a unique atmosphere. Visiting it is to step into a living story that continues to be written every day.
