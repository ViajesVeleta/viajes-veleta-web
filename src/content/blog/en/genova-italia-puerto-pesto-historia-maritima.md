The capital of Liguria is much more than a port; it is the largest seaport in **Italy** and the undisputed realm of *pesto* and *focaccia*. In **Genoa**, all stories are linked to the sea. The homeland of seafarers and, as believed, the birthplace of Christopher Columbus, the city proudly displays the wealth amassed over centuries of maritime trade.

Its **Old Port** (Porto Antico) is today the vibrant heart of the city, where modern icons like the Aquarium and the Biosphere coexist with splendid palaces and lively squares. Walking through its old port, which once controlled the Mediterranean, is to enjoy an unforgettable *passeggiata* amidst the glorious past of one of history's most powerful maritime republics.
