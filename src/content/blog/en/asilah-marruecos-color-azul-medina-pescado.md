I hesitated quite a bit over the title for this entry. What was more appropriate: the name of the city or the color that dominates its homes? In the end, I decided on the latter: **Blue**.

**Asilah** is a gem located in northern Morocco, just 50 km from Tangier. It boasts some of the country's best beaches and, without a shadow of a doubt, the best fish you can taste in the region. Its proximity to the Iberian Peninsula, its affordable prices, and the undeniable charm of its medina make it a highly recommended travel destination.

Photography enthusiasts will find an inexhaustible source of unique images here. In its streets, white, green, and ocher tones blend harmoniously, but it is blue that dominates absolutely. It is as if the city had become enamored with the intense color of the surrounding sea and decided to dress in it forever.
