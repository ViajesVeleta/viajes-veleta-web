There are a thousand and one reasons to visit **Prague**. Become one of the most beautiful cities in the Old Continent, the capital of the Czech Republic feels like it was taken out of a fairy tale. Its historic center, a World Heritage Site, bears witness to a past marked by the Kingdom of Bohemia.

Wandering through its narrow streets, crossing the legendary **Charles Bridge**, getting lost in the bowels of its impressive castle, and savoring a good *pivo* (beer) are pleasures that every traveler should experience. Prague is the city that saw the birth of Kafka and where every corner holds a legend.
