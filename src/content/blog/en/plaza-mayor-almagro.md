The historic town of **Almagro** is a journey back in time to the Spanish Golden Age. Its **Plaza Mayor**, of medieval origin, was renovated in the 16th century under the influence of the Fugger family, Swiss-German bankers to Charles V, who left their mark on the characteristic green glass galleries.

But Almagro is also the capital of classical theater, home to the famous **Corral de Comedias**, a unique gem that has remained intact since the 17th century. Strolling through its arcades and discovering its Renaissance palaces is an essential experience on any route through La Mancha.
