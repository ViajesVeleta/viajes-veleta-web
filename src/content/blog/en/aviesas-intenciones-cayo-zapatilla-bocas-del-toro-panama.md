Not everything in travel is about solemn landscapes and historical monuments; sometimes, the fun arises from shared moments and camaraderie. In this photograph, we see Pablo leaning against a palm tree on **Cayo Zapatilla**, a small, virgin island in the **Bocas del Toro** archipelago in Panama.

Who knows if he has sinister intentions or is simply enjoying the tropical shade? What's certain is that this corner of the Panamanian Caribbean invites playfulness and absolute disconnection. Its white-sand beaches and crystal-clear waters are the perfect setting to leave worries behind and surrender to the simplicity of an afternoon under the sun. A small fragment of happiness in a paradise that seems designed for laughter.
