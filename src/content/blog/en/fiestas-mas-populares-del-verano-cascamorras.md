**Cascamorras: Summer Traditions**

Today I leave you with a link to an article from **El País** where they talk about common summer festivals in Spain, featuring the **Cascamorras** (Baza and Guadix).

*The Cascamorras is more than just a festival; it's a centuries-old tradition where a character from Guadix tries to recover the image of the Virgen de la Piedad in Baza. The catch? He must remain clean, which is impossible as thousands of locals cover him in black paint in a joyful and chaotic battle. It's an explosion of energy and culture that defines the spirit of Andalusia in summer.*
