**Guided tours of the Cathedral of Santa María in Vitoria**

The spectacular restoration of this Gothic church, renamed the cathedral of Vitoria-Gasteiz in the 19th century, allows discovering its best-kept secrets, archaeological excavations and restoration works through a suggestive guided tour. Visitors will learn about the construction phases of the building, its polychrome portico, as well as the details of the history of the cathedral and the origin of Vitoria-Gasteiz. It was declared a World Heritage Site in the context of the inland northern Camino de Santiago. The **Cathedral of Santa María** has seduced a multitude of visitors, as well as internationally renowned writers such as Ken Follett, who was inspired by the cathedral to write the second part of *The Pillars of the Earth*.
