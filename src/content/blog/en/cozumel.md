**Cozumel** is a largely undeveloped Mexican island in the Caribbean Sea, is a popular cruise ship port of call famed for its scuba diving. At Arrecifes de Cozumel National Park, there are points of a section of the Mesoamerican Reef and the Museo Subacuático de Arte’s submerged sculptures.
