Designated as part of the transnational site of the **Great Spa Towns of Europe**, Karlovy Vary represents the golden age of thermal cures on the continent. From the 18th century to the early 20th, this Czech city was organized around its mineral springs, creating an urban environment of sumptuous beauty.

Strolling under its grand colonnades and decorated galleries is to travel back to an era of architectural and medicinal splendor. The city features numerous gardens, residential mansions, and infrastructures specifically designed for the enjoyment of thermal waters. During our group trip, we were able to admire the elegance of this destination that remains a benchmark for balneotherapy and European social life.
