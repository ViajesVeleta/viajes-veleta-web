**Ferrara** is one of the gems of the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna. This prosperous and quiet city still maintains the urban planning of its years of splendor, when it hosted the Este court between the 13th and 16th centuries.

The best way to visit is by strolling slowly through its historic center. From the train station, the path to the heart of the city is dotted with bourgeois villas in the *liberty* style. Once in the center, monuments such as the **Este Castle**, the **Palazzo dei Diamanti**, or the **Schifanoia Museum** justify the trip alone.
