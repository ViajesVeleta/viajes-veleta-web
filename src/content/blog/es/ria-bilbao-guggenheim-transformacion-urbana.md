La apertura del **Guggenheim** supuso el impulso definitivo para cambiarle el color a una urbe que hoy es ejemplo de vanguardia.

El museo no es solo el lugar más visitado de Euskadi, sino el corazón de una ría imparable que demuestra cómo la cultura puede ser el agente más eficaz para crear destinos sostenibles. Pasear por sus márgenes es ser testigo de un pasado industrial convertido en un presente lleno de vida, arte y modernidad.

The capital of Biscay has shown the world how it is possible to reinvent itself. After the shipyards closed in 1988, the **Bilbao** estuary sought its new destiny and found it in the form of a museum. The opening of the **Guggenheim** was the definitive impulse to change the color of a city that today is an example of the cutting edge.

The museum is not only the most visited place in Euskadi, but the heart of an unstoppable estuary that demonstrates how culture can be the most effective agent for creating sustainable destinations. Walking along its banks is to witness an industrial past transformed into a present full of life, art, and modernity.
