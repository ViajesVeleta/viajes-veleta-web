    On the shores of the **Mediterranean** lies **Sicily**, an amazing Italian island that easily falls in love and seduces. A tour of the Italian island of Sicily is a vibrant experience that combines **ancient culture**, **breathtaking natural landscapes**, and **unforgettable gastronomy**.

    From the elegant **Palermo** to the charming **Taormina**, the journey takes you through baroque cities like **Ragusa** and **Noto**, the **Greek temples of Agrigento**, **Segesta** with its Doric temple and beautiful panoramic views, and the imposing **Mount Etna**, one of the most active in the world. Are you in?

## Itinerary

<Timeline mode="alternate">
  <TimelineItem date="Day 1" title="Guadix - Granada / Barcelona - Catania" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/vueling">
    Departure from **Guadix** by coach at 06:00 (time pending reconfirmation) towards **Granada**. Presentation at the airport 90 minutes before departure. Check-in and boarding on a regular flight to **Catania**, via **Barcelona**. Arrival, reception, and transfer to the hotel. In the afternoon, **guided tour of the city**. **Dinner at the hotel or nearby restaurant**. Accommodation.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 2" title="Catania - Taormina - Catania" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/taormina">
    **Breakfast**. Departure to **Mount Etna** (subject to weather conditions), the highest and most active volcano in Europe (3,345 meters): the bus will arrive at **Sapienza Refuge** at 1,800 meters. Visit the extinct craters, called **"Crateri Silvestri"**. **Lunch en route**. Continuation to **Taormina**, located on the peak of the picturesque **Mount Tauro** rock (204 m). Visit to discover the romantic alleys of the city or the famous **Greek Theatre**, offering magnificent views of both Etna and the Ionian Sea. Return to the hotel. **Accommodation**.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 3" title="Catania - Syracuse - Noto - Ragusa" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/ragusa">
    **Breakfast**. Departure to **Syracuse**, the largest city of antiquity. We will visit the **Archaeological Park**, the **"Neapolis"**, and the **Duomo of Ortigia**. Continuation to **Noto**, the capital of Sicilian Baroque, where we will admire its grand, fully renovated cathedral and walk its streets full of architectural treasures. **Lunch**. Technical stop in **Modica**, famous for its chocolate, to end the day with free time in **Ragusa**, with a walk through the beautiful historic center. **Accommodation**.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 4" title="Ragusa - Piazza Armerina - Agrigento" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/templo-concordia">
    **Breakfast**. Departure to **Piazza Armerina**. Visit the splendid **Villa Romana del Casale**, a luxurious villa in the heart of Sicily, a prime example of the Roman period where you can admire precious mosaics depicting the customs of the time. **Lunch**. Continue to **Agrigento**, "the most beautiful city of mortals," to visit the famous and unique **"Valley of the Temples"**. **Accommodation**.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 5" title="Agrigento - Cefalú - Palermo" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/cefalu">
    **Breakfast**. **Cefalú** is a charming coastal town that offers visitors a wonderful artistic display of light and colors. **Guided tour** through narrow alleys that preserve their medieval charm. We will visit the **Norman Cathedral** (cathedral only, no towers) and the **Medieval Laundry**. **Lunch en route**. Arrival in **Palermo**. **Accommodation**.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 6" title="Palermo - Erice - Segesta - Palermo" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/erice">
    **Breakfast**. Departure to **Erice**, a medieval town 750 meters above sea level with Norman urban planning and Arabic organization. Famous for its **traditional sweets**. **Lunch**. Continue to western Sicily to visit **Segesta** and the **Doric Temple**. Return to the hotel in Palermo. **Accommodation**.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 7" title="Palermo - Monreale - Palermo" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/monreale">
    **Breakfast**. Morning departure to **Monreale** to visit the **cathedral** and its **cloister**. Return to Palermo. **Lunch**. In the afternoon, **guided walk through the historic center**, visiting monuments like the **Palatine Chapel**, **Palermo Cathedral**, or the famous **Pretoria Square**. Visit the **Martorana Church** and **Santa Caterina**. **Accommodation**.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem date="Day 8" title="Palermo - Barcelona / Granada - Guadix" image="assets/europa/italia/sicilia/palermo">
    **Breakfast**. Free time until the agreed time for the transfer to the airport and return flight to Spain. Arrival in **Granada** and transfer by bus to **Guadix**.
  </TimelineItem>
</Timeline>
