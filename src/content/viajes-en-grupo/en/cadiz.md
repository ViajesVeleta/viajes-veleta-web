A four-day / three-night cultural and gastronomic coach tour. A journey through the lands of Cadiz and the Grazalema mountains where we will enjoy an experience of contrasts: from the golden beaches of the Atlantic to the lush Spanish fir forests.

The white villages, such as Zahara de la Sierra or Grazalema, dazzle among mountains and ravines. The gastronomy combines the flavor of the sea with the aromas of the mountains. Winding roads invite you to get lost among green landscapes and infinite viewpoints.

It is a route that blends history, nature, and Andalusian authenticity in every corner. During the tour, we will visit the cities of El Puerto de Santa María, Conil and Vejer de la Frontera, Coto de Doñana, Sanlúcar de Barrameda, Grazalema, and Setenil de las Bodegas.

## Itinerary

- **Day 1**: Departure to Cadiz and visit to El Puerto de Santa María.
- **Day 2**: Route through the White Villages: Grazalema and Zahara de la Sierra.
- **Day 3**: Nature in Coto de Doñana and Sanlúcar de Barrameda.
- **Day 4**: Vejer de la Frontera and return.
